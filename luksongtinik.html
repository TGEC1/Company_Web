Luksong Tinik

Game Overview and How to Play

How to Play Luksong Tinik

Definition of Luksong Tinik

Luksong Tinik is a traditional Filipino game where players jump over two people who are crouching down with their legs spread apart, simulating thorns. It requires agility and coordination to jump over the "thorns" without touching them.

How to Play

The game starts with two players crouching and spreading their legs apart, forming a "thorn" formation. Other players take turns jumping over the "thorns." As the game progresses, the height of the "thorns" is gradually increased by having the crouching players raise their legs higher, making it more difficult to jump over them.
