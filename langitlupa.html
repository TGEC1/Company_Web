Langit Lupa

Game Overview and How to Play

How to Play Langit Lupa

Definition of Langit Lupa

Langit Lupa is a traditional Filipino game where two teams are divided into "Langit" (Heaven) and "Lupa" (Earth). The "Langit" team attempts to cross the field to the other side, while the "Lupa" team tries to tag them. The game is based on speed, strategy, and teamwork.

How to Play

In Langit Lupa, the "Langit" team tries to cross the field without being tagged by the "Lupa" team; if tagged, they return to the start, and the game ends when all "Langit" players either get tagged or cross successfully.
