Takyan

Larong Pinoy Traditional Game

How to Play Takyan

Definition of Takyan

Takyan is a traditional Filipino game where players use their feet to keep a small object, such as a coin or a rubber ball, in the air. It is similar to hacky sack but is played with more diverse techniques, including kicks, foot taps, and even head or knee touches.

How to Play

In Takyan, the objective is to keep the object off the ground by using your feet. The object is often a coin tied to a string or a small rubber ball. Players take turns attempting to keep it in the air, using any part of their body, but the main focus is on using the feet.
