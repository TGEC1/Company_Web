Tumbang Preso

Game Overview and How to Play

How to Play Tumbang Preso

Definition of Tumbang Preso

Tumbang Preso is a traditional Filipino outdoor game that is typically played by children. It involves knocking over a can with a slipper (or sandal) while trying to avoid being tagged by a player. It is similar to games like dodgeball but requires skill and speed in throwing and evading.

How to Play

In Tumbang Preso, one player is chosen to be the player, who is tasked with guarding the can placed at the center of a designated playing area. The rest of the players take turns attempting to knock over the can by throwing a slipper at it. Once the can is knocked over, players must quickly run to a designated safe zone without being tagged by the player.
