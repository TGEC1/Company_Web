Patentiro

Game Overview and How to Play

How to Play Patentiro

Definition of Patentiro

Patintero is a popular Filipino outdoor game that involves two teams. One team is tasked with creating "lines" across the playing area, while the other team tries to break through these lines. The game is often played on streets, playgrounds, or fields, and it requires strategy, speed, and agility.

How to Play

The game is played with two teams. The defending team will create a series of lines across the field. The attacking team must try to break through these lines without being tagged by the defenders. Players who get tagged are out of the game, while those who successfully cross the lines score a point for their team.
